Admissions

The BMDS Lab welcomes applications from talented and motivated students who are passionate about applying artificial intelligence, machine learning, and data science to biomedical and health-related problems. We offer research opportunities for undergraduate, MSc, and PhD students at Brock University.

PhD Admissions

The lab typically offers well-supported PhD positions annually. Applicants with backgrounds in computer science, machine learning, data science, bioinformatics, biological sciences, or related fields are encouraged to apply.

Please apply through Brock University's official graduate admissions portal and indicate Dr. Yifeng Li as your intended supervisor. You may also email your CV and transcripts to Dr. Li for preliminary contact.

MSc Admissions

Our Lab also offers well-supported MSc student positions annually. We encourage strong candidates from relevant disciplines such as Computer Science, Machine Learning, Data Science, Bioinformatics, Biological Sciences, or Biotechnology to reach out.

Prospective PhD applicants are encouraged to submit their materials through Brock University's official application system and indicate Dr. Yifeng Li as their intended supervisor. Applicants may also choose to send their CV and transcripts to Dr. Li for preliminary contact.

Undergraduate Opportunities

Research opportunities for undergraduate students at Brock University may be available through courses such as COSC 3P99, COSC 4F90, BIOL 4F90, or BIOL 4F91. Students with a strong interest in machine learning, biomedical data science, or bioinformatics are encouraged to reach out Dr. Li. Preference will be given to students with strong academic performance and a clear interest in the lab's research.
